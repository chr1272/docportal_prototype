Fire Alarm Control Panel - Exchange

The exchange of the Fire Alarm Control Panel consists of a procedure for removal and a procedure for installation.

  • Prior to commencing any work inside the Fire Alarm Control Panel, switch off the power at the fire alarm circuit breaker in the MDB. Ensure, the standby power (lead acid batteries) is disconnected, too.
