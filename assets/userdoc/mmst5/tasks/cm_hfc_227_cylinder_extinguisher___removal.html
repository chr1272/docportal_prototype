HFC-227 Cylinder Extinguisher - Removal

Removal of the HFC-227 Cylinder Extinguisher.

  1. Switch off the Fire Suppression System.
  2. Unscrew and remove the Manual Actuator and the Electrical Actuator assembly (see item 1 and 2 in Figure 1) by turning the Knurled Nut (see item 6 in Figure 1) counter clockwise.

  3. Remove the screw (see item 1 in Figure 2).

  4. Remove the black rubber cap.
  5. Note down the cabling and the connectors (see item 2 in Figure 2).
  6. Remove the connectors.
  7. Disconnect the HFC-227 pipe by turning the nut anti-clockwise (see item 5 in Figure 1).
  8. Remove the screw from the holder (see item 3 in Figure 3).
