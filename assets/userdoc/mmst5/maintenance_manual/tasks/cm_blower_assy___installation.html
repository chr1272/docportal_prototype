Blower Assy - Installation

Installation of the Blower Assy.

  1. Slide in the new unit - a horizontal slot on the left and on the right side will guide the assembly.
  2. Make sure, that the cable follows the unit in.
  3. Secure its position by mounting grub screws on each side.
  4. Mount the connector on the Power Supply Unit (PSU).
  5. Turn on power to the transceiver with the power switch on the PSU.
  6. Switch “Mains On” with the Radar Service Application (if needed, select antenna rotation, Tx and profile).
