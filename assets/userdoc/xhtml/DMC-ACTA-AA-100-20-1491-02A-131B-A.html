Blue Force Track information

Carry out the following procedure to get information about a Blue Force track.

Responsibility Field Unit Operator
Prerequisites N/A

  1. Select a Blue Force track on the map.

  2. Double-press on this track or on the track label.

    The [Track Information] panel is displayed.

    For the selected track, the following characteristics are given:

    • The identifier (Track ID)

    • The name (Track Name)

    • The type (Identification)

    • The status (operational, not operational, panic) (Status)

    • The indication that the track is inside the TETRA coverage area or not (In Coverage)

    • The position (Location)

    • The direction (Direction)

    • The speed (Speed)

    • The date and time of the last change (Last Update)

    Note: The tablet laptop is connected to TETRA network in the vehicle, and to the fixed network when in docked mode.

  3. Press the button to center the map on the track.
