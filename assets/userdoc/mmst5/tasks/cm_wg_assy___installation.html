WG Assy - Installation

Installation of the Waveguide Assy.

  1. Insert the new Waveguide Assy.
    Ensure, that the top flange is guided properly through the circular cut-out in the top of the cabinet and fix the new o-ring (see Figure 4).
  2. Mount and tighten the four screws which are holding the top flange to the cabinet (see red circles in Figure 4) and mount the Waveguide Connector.
  3. Check, that the o-ring is placed correctly (see item 1 in Figure 4).
  4. Tighten the four screws, which are holding the unit to the back (see red circles in Figure 5).
  5. Mount a new Cable Strap to fix the cables to the cable tray at the bottom of the Waveguide Assy (see Figure 3).
  6. Mount and tighten the Connectors X3, X5, X2 and X4 (see red circles in Figure 1).
  7. Remove the Termination on X14 of RxTx (see Figure 2).
  8. Mount the small cable between X6 from the Waveguide Assy and X14 from the RxTx (see Figure 1).
  9. Mount and tighten the Connectors X1, X10, X11, X12 and X13 on the RxTx (see yellow cirles in Figure 1).
  10. Mount the SSPA (see SSPA - Installation).
  11. Mount the Blower Assy (see Blower Assy - Installation).
  12. Mount the Waveguide SSPA with a Screwdriver, flexible, 6 mm.
  13. Turn on power with the power switch on the PSU.
  14. Switch “Mains On” with the Radar Service Application (if needed, select antenna rotation, Tx and profile).
