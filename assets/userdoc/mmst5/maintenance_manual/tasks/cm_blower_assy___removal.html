Blower Assy - Removal

Removal of the Blower Assy.

  1. Stop antenna rotation (if active) with the Radar Service Application.
  2. Switch “Mains Off” with the Radar Service Application.
  3. Turn off power to the transceiver with the power switch on the Power Supply Unit (PSU).
  4. On the PSU, remove the connection to the Blower Assy - the location of the connector is shown in Figure 1. If the screw is tightened too hard, use the Allen key, 2 mm.
    Figure 1. Connector
  5. Remove the two grub screws on the left and on the right side of the Blower Assy (see Figure 2) with the Allen key, 2 mm.
    Figure 2. Grub screws
  6. Pull out the Blower Assy together with its cable.
