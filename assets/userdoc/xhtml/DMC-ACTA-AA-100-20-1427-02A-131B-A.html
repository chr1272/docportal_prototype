Display a Task Order (adhoc task)

Responsibility Field Unit Operator
Prerequisites The task order was acknowledged.
Check

N/A

  1. In the information bar, press the current task button .

    The [ADHOC TASK] dialog box is displayed.

    It contains information related to the current adhoc task. It also includes five tabs and two buttons.

  2. Press the tab to display the instructions for the task.

  3. Press the tab to display the routes to follow for the task (the waypoints).

  4. Press the tab to display the primary and secondary targets of the task.

  5. Press the tab to display the important objects linked to the task.

  6. Press the tab to display the other teams involved in the same event.

  7. Press the button to zoom and center the map in order to display the elements linked to the current task.

  8. Press the button to center the map on the point where the event occurred.

    Tip: Pressing on an item in each list (waypoints, targets, teams, ...) will center the display on it.
