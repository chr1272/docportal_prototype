Area Of Responsibility (AOR)

Basically, the border area is organized in sectors under the responsibility of a headquarter. A Field Unit is assigned to a sector. Each sector is attached to a headquarter (HQ). The Sector Headquarter (SHQ) reports to a Regional Headquarter (RHQ) that reports to the National Headquarter (NHQ).

The AOR is a sector under the responsibility of a headquarter within which operations are created, assigned to field units, and whose progress is monitored.

Warning: AOR can be several sectors for a RHQ.
