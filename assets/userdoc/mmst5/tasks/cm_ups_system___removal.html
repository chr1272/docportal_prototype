UPS System - Removal

Removal of the UPS System.

  1. Note or record the wiring of the UPS.
  2. Remove all cables from the UPS.
  3. Remove the UPS battery modules (see UPS_Battery_Kit - Exchange).
  4. Remove the screws (see Figure 1).
    Figure 1. UPS Chassis Screws
  5. With one person on each side, slide the chassis out of the rail and put it down to the floor.
  6. Remove the mounting ears from the chassis (see Figure 2).
    Figure 2. UPS Chassis Mounting Ears
