Safety recommendations

The following warnings and cautions are generally applicable when working on the site.

CAUTION
Work in pairs
When working on a tower site, always work in pairs.
CAUTION
Safety gear
When working on a tower site, always wear a hard hat and safety shoes. When climbing up the tower, always wear the safety harness.
DANGER
Hazardous materials, energy sources
Before working on any equipment, be aware of the hazardous materials or energy sources and be familiar with standard practices for preventing accidents.
Read the the manufacturer documentation before connecting any equipnet to a power source.
Warning
Do not open
Do not open or disassemble any equipment unless explicitly instructed to do so by the documentation.
Warning
Heavy objects
Never attempt to lift an object that might be too heavy to be lift by an individual.
CAUTION
Loose clothing
Do not wear loose clothing, jewelry (including rings and chains), or other items that could get caught in the equipment. Fasten your tie or scarf and sleeves.
Warning
Food, liquids, tobacco
Drinking, eating or smoking is prohibited inside all rooms hosting technical equipment.
