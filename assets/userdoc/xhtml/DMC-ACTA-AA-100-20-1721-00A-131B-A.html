Distance Measurement

To calculate the distance between two points, proceed as follows.

Responsibility Field Unit Operator
Prerequisites N/A

  1. From the menu, press the button.

    The following window is displayed.

  2. Press a point on the map.

    Each time a point is pressed on the map, a new line is added in the [TERRAIN DISTANCES] window.

    Distances are automatically calculated.

    Azimuth and angle are also automatically added in this window.
