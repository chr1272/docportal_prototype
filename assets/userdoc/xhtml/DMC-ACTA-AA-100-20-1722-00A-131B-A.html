Surface Measurement

To calculate distance perimeters and surface between three or more points on the map, proceed as follows.

Responsibility Field Unit Operator
Prerequisites N/A

  1. From the menu, press the button.

    The following window is displayed.

  2. Press a point on the map.

    Each time a point is pressed on the map, a new line is added in the [TERRAIN SURFACE] window.

    Distances, perimeter and surface are automatically calculated.

    Azimuth and angle are also automatically added in this window.
