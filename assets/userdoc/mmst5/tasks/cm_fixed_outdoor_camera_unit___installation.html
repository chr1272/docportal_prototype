Fixed Outdoor Camera Unit - Installation

Installation of the Fixed Outdoor Camera Unit.

  1. Mount the replacement camera into the camera housing.

  1. Slightly tilt the camera/lens tray assembly and slide it in under the rail (which is located under the heater, on the right side of the housing).
  2. Lower the tray, so that the holes at the side fit over the two screws of the housing.
  3. Slide the entire assembly forward (approximately 5 mm (0.2 in.) from the front of the

    window).

  4. Tighten the two screws, to lock the tray in place.
    Figure 1. Camera mounting

  1. Connect the network and the power cable.
  2. Check, if the camera is functional.
