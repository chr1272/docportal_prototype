Relay - Removal

Removal of the Relay.

  1. Open the front doors of the upmast cabinet to get access to the relays (see Figure 1).
    Figure 1. Location of relays
    To remove the two middle relays it is necessary to remove the left or right outer relay first.
  2. Pull the relay approximately 5 mm out at the lower site (see item 2 in Figure 2).
    Figure 2. Relay
    1
    Relay base

  3. Carefully remove the capacitor (see item 3 in Figure 2).
  4. Swing the spring down.
  5. Remove the relay from the base.
