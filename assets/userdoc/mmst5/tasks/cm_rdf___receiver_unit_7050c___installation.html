RDF - Receiver Unit 7050C - Installation

Installation of the RDF - Receiver Unit 7050C.

  1. Slide the replacement Unit into the rack.
  2. Refasten the four screws on the front side.
  3. Install the grounding cable at the back side (see Figure 2).
  4. Install all cables at the front side as marked (see Figure 1).
  5. Switch the Power Supply ON (see item 3 in Figure 1).
  6. Switch the Receiver Unit ON (see item 1 in Figure 1).
  7. Close the front and rear doors of the upmast cabinet.
