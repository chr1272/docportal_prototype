Zone Information

Carry out the following procedure to get more information about a zone.

Responsibility Field Unit Operator
Prerequisites N/A

  1. Select a zone on the map.

  2. Double-press inside this zone or on the zone label.

    The [ZONE INFORMATION] panel is displayed.

    For the selected zone, the following characteristics are given:

    • The name (NAME)

    • The category (CATEGORY)

    • The date and time of the last change (LAST UPDATE TIME)

    • A brief description (DESCRIPTION)

  3. Press the button to zoom in on the current zone.
