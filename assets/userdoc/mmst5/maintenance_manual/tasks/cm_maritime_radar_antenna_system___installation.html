Maritime Radar Antenna System - Installation

Installation of the Maritime Radar Antenna System.

  1. Lift the Scanner with the two Lifting Yokes and the Mounted Slings, and fasten the enclosed nuts to the yoke (see Figure 4 and Figure 5).
  2. When the Scanner is placed on the gearbox, mount eight new Nuts with new Washers.
    Terma part no. 200682-007 and 232439-008, respectively.
    1. Fasten the two nuts, pointing towards the front and back in the antenna radiation direction, with a tightening torque of 60 Nm.
    2. Fasten the remaining six screws pointing to the sides with a tightening torque of 50 Nm (see Figure 6).
    3. Do NOT remove the slings before the Scanner is fastened to the gearbox.
    4. To lock the nuts in place, bend the washers with a Hammer and a Pin punch (see Figure 7).
  3. Mount and fasten the four Finger Screws (see Figure 3) to assemble the Waveguide in the scanner and the Waveguide in the installation.
    For the Reinforced Scanners, the center Waveguide is rectangular and the finger screws are substituted by normal Waveguide screws.
  4. Mount the Cover Plates (see Figure 1 and Figure 2).
