Battery - Removal

Removal of the Battery.

  1. Shut off power to the light fixture at the circuit breaker.
  2. Remove the translucent plastic cover.
  3. Remove the fluorescent tubes by twisting the tubes in anti-clockwise direction.
  4. Remove the metal cover plate.
  5. Record the cable connections.
  6. Remove the cable connections by pushing the cable pin socket.
  7. Remove all cables.
  8. Remove the two screws, which are fixing the Battery in the lamp fixture.
  9. Remove the Battery.
