Smoke/Heat Detector - Removal

Removal of the Smoke/Heat Detector.

  1. Identify which Smoke/Heat Detector is faulty, by using the appropriate tools (Smoke test spray or Heat gun).
  2. Remove the Smoke/Heat Detector from the base (see item 1 and 3 in Figure 1).
