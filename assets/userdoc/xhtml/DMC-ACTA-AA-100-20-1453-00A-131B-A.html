Confirm a Track

The Field Unit Operator can send the confirmation that he has observed a track or not.

Responsibility Field Unit Operator
Prerequisites N/A

  1. Select a track on the map.

  2. Double-press the track.

    The [TRACK INFORMATION] dialog box is displayed.

  3. Press the [CONFIRM] button. The [CONFIRM TRACK] dialog box is displayed.

  4. Select the information related to the target:

    • To specify whether or not the target is visible

    • To define the date and time of the observation

    • To give additional information related to the observation

  5. Press the [SEND] button to send the confirmation.
