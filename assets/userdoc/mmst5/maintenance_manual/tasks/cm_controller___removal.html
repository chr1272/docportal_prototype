Controller - Removal

Removal of the Controller.

  1. Disconnect the cables from the CX9020 controller (see Figure 1).
  2. Remove the bar clips on the top side and on the underside (see Figure 1).
  3. To release the terminal, pull the orange terminal release.
  4. Pull the CX9020 controller slightly until it releases from the mounting rail.
