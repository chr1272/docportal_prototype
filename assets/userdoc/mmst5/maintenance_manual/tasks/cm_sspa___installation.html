SSPA - Installation

Installation of the SSPA.

  1. Mount the new unit by fixing it in the remaining screw (see yellow circle in Figure 3).
  2. Fasten the unit to the back with the other seven screws (see Figure 3).
  3. Mount the Harting Connector in the PSU (see Figure 2).
  4. Mount the D-Sub Connector X4 and the three SMA Connectors X2, X3 and X5 (see Figure 1).
  5. Mount the Blower Assy.
  6. Mount the Waveguide SSPA.
  7. Turn on power to the transceiver with the power switch on the PSU.
  8. Switch “Mains On” with the Radar Service Application (if needed, select antenna rotation, Tx and profile).
  9. Make a PSAT calibration of the SSPA (see SSPA - PSAT Calibration).
