WG, SSPA - Installation

Installation of the WG, SSPA.

  1. Mount the new waveguide assembly, mount and tighten the eight screws using the Screwdriver, flexible, 6 mm.
  2. Turn on power to the transceiver using the power switch on the power supply unit.
  3. Switch “Mains On” by means of the Radar Service application (if needed, select antenna rotation, Tx and profile).
