  2. Disconnect the cables from the Digital Input Terminal.
  3. To remove the terminal, release the secure lock on the mounting rail.
    Figure 2. Removal of the terminal
  4. Pull the terminal by its orange-colored lugs approximately 1 cm away from the mounting rail (see Figure 2).
    The mounting rail lock will be released automatically.
  5. Pull the terminal out of the bus terminal block.
  6. Hold the released terminal with thumb and index finger at the same time at the upper and lower grooved housing surfaces.
  7. Pull the terminal out of the bus terminal block.
