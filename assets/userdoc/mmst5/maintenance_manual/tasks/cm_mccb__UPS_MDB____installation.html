MCCB (UPS MDB) - Installation

Installation of the MCCB (UPS MDB).

  1. Insert the new MCCB into the UPS MDB.
  2. Refasten the two screws, that fix the MCCB to the steel frame (see item 3 in Figure 2).
  3. Reconnect all cables to the MCCB.
  4. Refasten the cover with the screw (see item 2 and 3 in Figure 1).
  5. Close the UPS MDB.
  6. Reconnect the output line of the UPS Module at the rear side of the rack.
  7. Switch the power for the Main Distribution Board (MDB) on.
