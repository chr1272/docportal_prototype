Battery (Server) - Removal

Removal of the Battery (Server).

  1. Remove the server.
  2. Move the server to a dust-free environment.
  3. Remove the two screws, which are fixing the top cover to the chassis (see item 1 in Figure 1).
  4. Lift up the cover.
  5. Remove the battery (see item 2 in Figure 1) from the motherboard.
