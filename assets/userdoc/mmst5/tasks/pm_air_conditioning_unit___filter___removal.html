Air Conditioning Unit - Filter - Removal

Removal of the Air Conditioning Unit - Filter.

  1. Switch both MDB circuit breakers for the Air Conditioning Unit to OFF.
  2. Remove the six screws from the Filter Access Cover of the Air Conditioning Unit (see Figure 1).
    Figure 1. Filter access cover (closed)
  3. Remove the Filter Access Cover (see Figure 2).
    Figure 2. Filter access cover (open)
  4. Remove the Filter from the Air Conditioning Unit (see Figure 3).
    Figure 3. Filter
