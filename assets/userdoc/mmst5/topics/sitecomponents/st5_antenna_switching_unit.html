Antenna Switching Unit (ASU)

The Antenna Switching Unit (ASU) switches channels between antenna arrays.

The RDF antenna is equipped with an outdoor Antenna Switching Unit "GEW Technologies GAS3000G". The purpose of the ASU is to route one of two antenna arrays to a single two channel output. The ASU is installed in a box at the antenna mast base.

Technical/Performance data:

  • Frequency range: 20 – 3000 MHz (3 band)
  • Number of channels per sub-array: 5
  • Antenna selection: Any 2 antennas per sub-array
  • Nominal H-plane beam-width: 360°
  • Nominal E-plane beam-width: 60°
  • Polarization: Vertical

All co-axial sockets are in use, a dust caps is only needed for the compass cable connector.
