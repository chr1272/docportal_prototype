Area Of Operation (AOO) and COP Status

AOOs have been defined to prevent the radio network from overloading. The AORs are divided into AOOs to limit the amount of information transmitted through the radio network.

There are three functional COP statuses:
  • Not limited (complete state)
  • Limited
  • Out of AOO

The COP status behaviour is given in the following table.

Table 1. Color code of the COP status

Color

Meaning

Complete state: the entire Mobile COP of the AOO is received.

Limited state: only a part of the Mobile COP of the AOO is received to reduce bandwidth consumption.

Only the essential information is transmitted (typically, all the information related to the own task).

Blue Force Tracking (BFT) is also transmitted.

The Mobile COP is unavailable since the Field Unit is out of any AOO.
