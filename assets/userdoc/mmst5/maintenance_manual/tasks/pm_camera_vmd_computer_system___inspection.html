Camera VMD Computer System - Inspection

This inspection procedure applies to the Camera VMD Computer (housed in the ground shelter rack) and the Radar Digitizing Unit Computer (installed in the upmast cabinet).

  • 2
  • Border guard
  • Basic
  • 15

  • Dry soft cloth

  1. DANGER
    DANGEROUS VOLTAGE!
    The power supply line must be connected for this procedure. Do not touch electrically live parts.
  2. CAUTION
    Dry and clean instrument
    Keep the instrument as dry and free from dirt and dust as possible.
  1. Make sure that the device
    • is free of damages and has no missing parts
    • is properly mounted and has no loose fittings
  2. Clean the entire back of the device. Clean all fan grill areas and outlets. Make sure that all air intakes are not blocked.
  3. Clean all surfaces.
  4. Check if the "Power On/Off LED" is illuminated in green.
  5. Check the correct connections of the computer:
    1. Service computer system
      • DVI port at DU - network cable - VGA/DVI adaptor - IQ module
      • LAN1 port at DU - network cable - LAN port at Ethernet Switch
      • USB1 port at DU - data cable - IQ module
      • Earthing connector at PDU - earthing cable - Grounding Bar
      • Pwr_01 port at DU - power cable - Rack PDU
      • Pwr_02 port at DU - power cable - Rack PDU
    2. VMD computer system
      Figure 1. Camera VMD computer
      • VGA port at DU - network cable - VGA/DVI adaptor - IQ module
      • LAN1 port at DU - data cable - GPUx
      • USB1 port at DU - data cable - IQ module
      • Earthing connector at PDU - earthing cable - Grounding Bar
      • Pwr_01 port at DU - power cable - Rack PDU
      • Pwr_02 port at DU - power cable - Rack PDU
