Home and Target Distance

These values represent the distance from the vehicle to the home and from the vehicle to the target.

The home position is usually the position of the vehicle base. The target position is provided by the HQC2 in a current task order.

Note: The home position is relative to the RRS where the Field Unit is normally deployed. If the Field Unit is re-deployed, the home location must be manually re-modified.

In the situation display frame (see Figure 2), the Field Unit Operator can press the blue arrow (2) to center the current map view on the home position or the red arrow (1) to center the current map view on the target position.
