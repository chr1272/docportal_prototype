Transformer/Capacitor - Installation

Installation of the Transformer/Capacitor.

  1. Installation of the Transformer
    1. Install the new Transformer with the screw (see item 6 in Figure 2).
    2. Connect the cables to the clamp (see item 4 in Figure 2) as noted down.
  2. Installation of the Capacitor
    1. Install the new Capacitor with the screw (see item 7 in Figure 2).
    2. Connect the Connector (see item 9 in Figure 2) to the Capacitor.
  3. Install the Aluminium Reflector with the three screws (see item 1 to 3 in Figure 2) with an X-point screwdriver, small.
  4. Install the Mercury bulb (see item 2 in Figure 1) by rotating the bulb in clockwise direction.
  5. Close the External Light with two screws (see item 1 in Figure 1).
  6. Turn on power to the light fixture at the circuit breaker.
