WG, SSPA - Removal

Removal of the WG, SSPA.

  1. Using the Radar Service application, stop antenna rotation (if active) and switch "Mains Off".
  2. Turn off power to the transceiver using the power switch on the power supply unit.
  3. Remove eight screws holding the waveguide assembly and remove it. Use the Screwdriver, flexible, 6 mm. See Figure 1
