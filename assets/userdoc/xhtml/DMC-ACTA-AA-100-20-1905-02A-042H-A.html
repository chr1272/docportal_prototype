List of Terms

This document uses the terms listed in the table below.

Table 1. List of terms

Term

Description

Command Center

The Command Center is the node responsible for operations.

  • Tactical Command Centers (TCC) are located in Sector Headquarters (SHQ).
  • Operational Command Centers (OCC) are located in Regional Headquarters (RHQ).
  • The Strategic Command Center (SCC) is located in the National Headquarter (NHQ).

Event

Decision based on an alert or by operator intention to perform response measures (Adhoc Event) or to start a preplanned Operation (Standard Event).

An Event consists of several attributes (Type, Source, Time, Location). Depending on the source and the event type, additional attributes are required (Track ID).

Field Unit

Any personnel and equipment of the own forces used to conduct operations.

Operation

Planned and defined action of a planned/routine or adhoc nature within the Area Of Responsibility (AOR), to be executed by one or more field units, under the command of one or more command centers.

Order

Specific type of activity assigned to a specific resource.

Typically the HQ sends an order to the Field Unit.

TACOM

Tactical Command

TACOM is the authority delegated to a commander to assign tasks to forces under his command for the accomplishment of the mission assigned by higher authority. TACOM includes the authority to delegate or retain TACON.

TACON

Tactical Control

TACON is the authority normally limited to the detailed and specified local direction of movement and maneuver of the tactical force to accomplish an assigned task. TACON does not provide organizational authority or administrative and support responsibilities.

Target

 Object defined by its location and requiring some surveillance or response actions.

Task

Response to an Event. The following objects can be defined for a task: target(s), waypoint(s), important object(s), field unit(s) assigned to the event.

Track

Representation of a moving physical object with its course and direction and additional parameters such as identification and classification.
