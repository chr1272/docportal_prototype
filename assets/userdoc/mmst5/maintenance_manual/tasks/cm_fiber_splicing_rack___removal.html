Fiber Splicing Rack - Removal

Removal of the Fiber Splicing Rack.

  1. Remove the patch-cords (see Figure 14).
  2. Remove the excess fibers around the spools (see Figure 13).
  3. Remove each fiber and connector from the panel (see Figure 12).
  4. Remove the panels from the enclosure (see Figure 11).
  5. Remove the cable from the grommet (see Figure 10).
  6. Remove the bend radius brackets (see Figure 9).
  7. Unscrew four screws and remove the enclosure from the rack (see Figure 8).
  8. Remove the ten washers and hex nuts from the sliding tray (see Figure 7).
  9. Remove the hex nut and washer (see Figure 6).
  10. Remove the spools from the enclosure (see Figure 5).
  11. Remove the strain relief bracket (see Figure 4).
  12. Remove the grommet(s) from the entry location of the fiber tray (see Figure 3).
  13. Disengage the cover (see Figure 2).
