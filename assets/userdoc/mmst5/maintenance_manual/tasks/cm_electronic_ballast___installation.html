Electronic Ballast - Installation

Installation of the Electronic ballast.

  1. Insert the new Electronic ballast.
  2. Refasten the two screws which fix the Electronic ballast in the lamp fixture.
  3. Connect all cables by pulling the cable pin socket.
  4. Refasten the metal cover plate.
  5. Refasten the fluorescent tubes by twisting the tubes in clockwise direction.
  6. Switch on power to the light fixture at the circuit breaker.
