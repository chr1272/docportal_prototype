Circuit Breaker - Removal

Removal of the Circuit Breaker.

  1. Switch the power for the Main Distribution Board (MDB) off.
  2. Open the MDB to gain access to the Circuit Breaker (see Figure 1).
    Figure 1. Circuit Breakers
    1
    Circuit Breaker
    2
    Circuit Breaker
  3. Note all cable connections to the Circuit Breaker.
  4. Remove the power cable of the Circuit Breaker IN and OUT.
  5. After removing the cables pull down the DIN rail clip and pull it out from the DIN rail.
  6. Remove the Circuit Breaker from the MDB.
