PSU (SCADA) - Removal

Removal of the PSU (SCADA) - Adelsystem flex28024a 24V DC.

  1. Remove the whole CX9020 Hardware Block, before the individual modules are disconnected.
  2. Release and remove the first Terminal, next to the Power Supply Unit (PSU) on the tophat rail.
  3. Note down the wiring.
  4. Remove all wiring from the PSU.
  5. Remove all wiring from the first Terminal.
  6. Pull the orange terminal release to release the first Terminal.
  7. Pull the first Terminal out.
  8. Release the CX9020 Hardware Block:
    1. Release the orange DIN rail bracket on the left-hand side of the device.
    2. Push back the lower hook.
      An audible click indicates that the device is released.
  9. Pull the terminal release of the PSU.
  10. Remove the PSU carefully from the tophat rail.
