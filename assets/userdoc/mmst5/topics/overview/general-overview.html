Site overview

Site type 5 (Cam, Radar, AIS, RDF) is composed of the following main components:

  • A tower designed and delivered by Airbus DS
  • A ground shelter delivered by Airbus DS (including the sensor monitoring rack)
  • Outdoor sensors equipment
    • Daylight & Infrared Camera with LRF
    • Maritime Radar
    • AIS
    • RDF (Radio Direction Finder)
  • Upmast cabinet
