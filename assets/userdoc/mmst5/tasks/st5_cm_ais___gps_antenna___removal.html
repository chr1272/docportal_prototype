AIS - GPS Antenna - Removal

Removal of the AIS - GPS Antenna.

  1. Switch off the AIS Base Station with the software and turn off the device power switch.
  2. Disconnect the cable connected to the GPS Antenna. If necessary, remove cable ties.
  3. Unscrew the GPS Antenna from the support.
