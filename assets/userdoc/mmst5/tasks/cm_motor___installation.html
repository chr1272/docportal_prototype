Motor - Installation

Installation of the Motor.

  1. Mount the new Motor with a new gasket between the Motor and the Gearbox.
    A gasket is delivered with the new Motor.
  2. Fasten the Motor with the nine bolts, spacer and nut, and washers (see Figure 1).
  3. Fill oil in the Gearbox until the right level is obtained (check in the inspection glass).
    If beneficial, use the opportunity to replace the oil.
  4. Mount all covers.
