SSPA - Removal

Removal of the SSPA.

  1. Stop antenna rotation (if active) with the Radar Service Application.
  2. Switch “Mains Off” with the Radar Service Application.
  3. Turn off power to the transceiver with the power switch on the >Power Supply Unit (PSU).
  4. Remove the Waveguide SSPA.
  5. Remove the Blower Assy.
  6. Unscrew and unplug D-Sub Connector X4 with a Screwdriver, 3,5 mm x 100 (see Figure 1).
  7. Unscrew and unplug the three SMA Connectors X2, X3 and X5 with a Torque wrench, 1 Nm (see Figure 1).
    Figure 1. Connectors
  8. On the PSU, unplug the Harting Connector which supplies the SSPA (see Figure 2).
    Figure 2. SSPA Assembly - Harting Connector
    1
    Harting Connector
  9. With a Screwdriver PH2 x 300 loosen seven of the eight screws, which are holding the SSPA Module to the cabinet. One screw on the left side (see yellow circle in Figure 3) should remain untouched.
    Figure 3. Screws
    The SSPA hangs now only at the one screw, which is still tightend.
  10. Lift the SSPA Module upwards and take it out.
  11. Place the SSPA Module into an antistatic bag.
