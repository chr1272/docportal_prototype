GPS Antenna (TRIMBLE)

The RDF system is equipped with a "Bullet III" GPS Antenna. The GPS antenna is mounted on a corrosion resistant socket, which itself is mounted on a flange at the RDF antenna. The GPS antenna connector is located inside the GPS antenna socket.

The GPS antenna features:

  • Gain 30 dB on input voltage 3.3 V DC
