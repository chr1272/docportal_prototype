RxTx - Installation

Installation of the RxTx.

  1. Insert the new RxTx.
  2. Mount and tighten the four screws which are holding the RxTx to the back of the cabinet (see red circles in Figure 4).
  3. On the RxTx Controller, mount and tighten the SMA Connectors X1 and X10 and the two D-Sub Connectors X12 and X14 with Screwdriver, 3,5 mm x 100 (see Figure 3).
  4. On the Waveguide Assy, tighten X2 with Torque wrench, 1 Nm and Extender for torque wrench.
  5. Remove the Termination on X14 of RxTx (see Figure 2).
  6. Mount the small cable between X6 from the Waveguide Assy and X14 from the RxTx (see Figure 1).
  7. On RxTx, mount and tighten all remaining SMA Connectors (X1 - X13) with Torque wrench, 1 Nm and Extender for torque wrench and two D-Sub Connectors (X15 - X16) with Screwdriver, 3,5 mm x 100 (see red circles in Figure 3).
  8. On the RxTx Controller, tighten the SMA Connectors X2 - X9 with Torque wrench, 1 Nm and Extender for torque wrench.
  9. Turn on power to the transceiver with the power switch on the PSU.
  10. Switch “Mains On” with the Radar Service Application (if needed, select antenna rotation, Tx and profile).
  11. Make a PSAT calibration of the SSPA (see RxTx - PSAT calibration).
