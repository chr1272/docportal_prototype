PC Controller Board, Battery - Installation

Installation of the Battery, lithium, for PC Controller Board.

  1. Place the new battery in the same position as the old battery.
  2. Fix the new battery with a cable strap to the PC Controller Board - cut the strap where necessary.
  3. Connect the red/black wire to the connector (see Figure 2).
  4. Mount the PC Controller Board (see Figure 1) in the crate.
  5. Turn on power to the transceiver with the power switch on the PSU.
  6. Set up date and time in the operational system.
  7. Switch “Mains On” with the Radar Service Application (if needed, select antenna rotation, Tx and profile).
