Internal Fire Alarm Sounder - Installation

Installation of the Fire Alarm Sounder.

  1. Replace the Fire Alarm Sounder.
  2. Set the dipswitches as shown in Figure 2.
  3. Connect the cables with a Flat screwdriver, small as noted down.
  4. Carefully plug in the sounder and turn it clockwise.
  5. Test the Fire Controls System to ensure the new Fire Alarm Sounder works properly.
  6. Switch the Fire Alarm System in the active mode.
