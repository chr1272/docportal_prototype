Relay - Installation

Installation of the Relay.

  1. Insert the relay in the base (see items 1 and 2 in Figure 2).
  2. Swing the spring over the relay.
  3. Lift the relay approximately 5 mm out at the lower site (see item 2 in Figure 2).
  4. Carefully insert the capacitor (see item 3 in Figure 2).
  5. Push the relay back into the base.
