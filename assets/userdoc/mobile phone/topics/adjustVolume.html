Adjusting the volume

You can adjust the volume level of your ring tone
  • Press the Volume UP key on the left side of your phone from the keyboard, to increase the volume.
  • Press the Volume DOWN key on the left side of your phone from the keyboard, to decrease the volume.
  • If you press Volume UP/Volume UP button during a call, the volume for phone conversation is adjusted.
