AIS - Base Station - Unit - Installation

Installation of the AIS - Base Station - Unit.

  1. Check, that the new unit is not damaged.
  2. Install the unit in the 19-inch rack on supporting rails or shelves. Do not slide the unit in completely, to have enough space to connect the cables to the unit.
  3. Connect the ground cables to the ground terminal of each unit labelled "Ground" before any other cabling is attached to the units. The ground cables shall be connected to a ground terminal in the rack or in the building.
  4. Before any antenna cables are attached, the VSWR value of the VHF installation must be checked to ensure that the antenna is properly installed:
    1. Connect the VSWR meter to the antenna cable.
    2. Verify, that the VSWR value is less than 2.0 : 1.
    The GPS installation cannot be checked by a VSWR meter as it is an active antenna and the impedance may vary depending on whether or not it is powered.
  5. Install the VHF antenna cable: Connect the VHF antenna cable to the connector labelled "RX/TX" on the rear panel of the FASS.
  6. Install the GPS antenna cable: Connect the GPS antenna cable to the terminal labelled "GPS" on the rear panel of the FASS.
  7. Network cable installation: No Ethernet cables shall be attached to the AIS Data Transfer Network at this point. Disconnect any cable connected to the AIS Data Transfer Network.
    The network will be configured and attached during setting to work.
  8. AC installation:
    1. Make sure that the power switch is in „0" state on the FASS.
    2. Connect the AC power supply cable to the connector labelled „Main Power".
    3. Connect the power cable to the AC outlet on site.
  9. DC installation (+24 V):
    1. Make sure that the power switch is in „0" state on the FASS.
    2. Connect the DC power supply cable to the connector labelled "+24 V DC Input".
    3. Connect the power cable to the +24 V DC outlet on site.
  10. Fasten the unit with appropriate screws and cage nuts.
  11. Switch the unit on.
