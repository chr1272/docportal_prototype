PSU (TERMA) - Installation

Installation of the PSU (TERMA).

  1. Insert the new Power Supply Unit (PSU) into the cabinet.
  2. Fasten all five screws (see Figure 2).
  3. Remount all connectors (see Figure 1).
  4. Turn on power to the transceiver with the power switch on the PSU.
  5. Switch “Mains On” with the Radar Service Application (if needed, select antenna rotation, Tx and profile).
