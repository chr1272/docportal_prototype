MCCB - Installation

Installation of the MCCB.

  1. Insert the new MCCB into the MDB.
  2. Refasten the two screws, that fix the MCCB to the steel frame (see item 2 in Figure 2).
  3. Reconnect all cables to the MCCB.
  4. Refasten the Lower Cover and the Upper Cover (see item 4 and 6 in Figure 1).
  5. Switch on the power with the Main Switch (see item 3 in Figure 1).
  6. Close the Inner Door and the Outer Door.
