Turning Unit Cover - Installation

Installation of the Turning Unit Cover.

  1. Installation of Top Cover
    1. Remove the O-ring and the four bolts (see Figure 1) and unmount the Top Cover.
      Figure 1. Cover 9
    2. Put on the Top Cover by lifting it up over the shaft.
    3. Refasten the four hexagon bolts (see Figure 2)
      Figure 2. Cover 8
    4. Put on the O-ring by pushing it in and down (see Figure 3 in reverse direction).
      Figure 3. Cover 7
  2. Installation of Left Side Cover and Right Side Cover
    1. Put on the Side Cover (see Figure 4 in reverse direction).
      Figure 4. Cover 6
  3. Installation of Encoder Cover and Motor Cover
    1. Put the cover back and refasten the four screws (see Figure 5).
      Figure 5. Cover 4
    2. Refasten the four hexagon bolts (see Figure 6).
      Figure 6. Cover 1
