  2. Switch off the power with the Main Switch (see item 3 in Figure 1).
  3. Remove the Lower Cover and the Upper Cover (see item 4 and 6 in Figure 1) by removing the screws, which hold the covers in place.
  4. Note all cable connections to theMCCB.
  5. Remove the screws (see item 1 in Figure 2) to remove the power cables of the MCCB IN and OUT.
  6. Remove the two screws, that fix the MCCB to the steel frame (see item 2 in Figure 2).
