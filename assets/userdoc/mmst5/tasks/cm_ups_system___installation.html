UPS System - Installation

Installation of the UPS System.

  1. If necessary, select the proper holes in the ground shelter rack for the positioning of the new UPS.
    The UPS rails are installed in positions 1 and 12 (see Figure 1).
    Figure 1. Holes of the UPS Rails
  2. If necessary loosen the hex nuts and extend the brackets to the desired length (see Figure 2).
    Figure 2. UPS Rails
  3. If necessary insert screws through the rack into the mounting rail and the front of each mounting bracket (see Figure 3).
    Figure 3. UPS Rear Rack
  4. If necessary install cage nuts or clip nuts into the rear of the rack (see Figure 4).
    Figure 4. UPS Rack Cage Nuts
  5. If necessary insert screws through the mounting rail into the cage nuts or clip nuts (see Figure 5).
    Figure 5. UPS Rear Rack Mounting Rail
  6. If necessary tighten the hex nuts (see Figure 6).
    Figure 6. Tightened UPS Rail
  7. With one person on each side of the UPS chassis, lift the chassis and lower it to the floor in front of the rack.
  8. Install the mounting ears on the chassis (see Figure 2).
  9. With one person on each side, lift the chassis to rail level and slide the chassis on the mounting rails.
  10. Attach the chassis to the rack with the supplied screws (see Figure 1).
  11. If you are using the rear mounting brackets, be sure that the tabs are fully inserted into the rear panel cutouts, then tighten the brackets.
  12. Install all cabling, mains input connection and outputs in accordance with your notes from the removal procedure.
