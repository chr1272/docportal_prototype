Waveguide Assembly

The Waveguide Assembly provides a special form of transmission line for microwaves.

The Waveguide Assembly is mounted between the Solid State Power Amplifier (SSPA) and the output flange to the antenna, which is located on top of the cabinet. The Waveguide Assembly includes various test and calibration ports.
