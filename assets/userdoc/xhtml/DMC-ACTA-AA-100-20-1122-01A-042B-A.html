Panic Button

The Panic button sends a panic alarm to the Headquarter Command and Control (HQC2).

Warning:

This action will shut down the terminal.

After a panic shutdown, the Field Unit turns to panic status: the operational data are erased, user login is not possible. To be able to log in, it is necessary to dock again in RRS/HQ to resynchronize the data in docked mode (please refer to Synchronization), then the status is switched back to not-operational.
