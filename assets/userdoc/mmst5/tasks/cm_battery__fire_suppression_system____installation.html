Battery (Fire Suppression System) - Installation

Installation of the Battery 12V/7Ah (fire suppression system).

  1. Insert the new battery.
  2. Reinstall the batteries cables (as shown in Figure 2).
  3. Turn the batteries in an upright position.
  4. Make sure that no "Power Fault" is shown on the control panel of the Fire Suppression System.
  5. Close the panel of the Fire Suppression System.
