Computer (Service Computer, VMD, RDU) - Filter - Removal

Removal of the computer air filter. This procedure applies to the Service computer, the VMD computer and the Radar Digitizing Unit computer.

  1. Open the front door with the server key.
    The front air filter is located on the left.
  2. Loosen the knurled-head screws.
  3. Pull the air filter bezel forward and down.
  4. Remove the air filter.
