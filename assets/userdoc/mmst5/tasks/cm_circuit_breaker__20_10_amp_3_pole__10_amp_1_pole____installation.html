Circuit Breaker (20/10 Amp 3 pole, 10 Amp 1 pole) - Installation

Installation of the Circuit breaker (20/10 Amp 3 pole, 10 Amp 1 pole).

  1. Insert the new Circuit Breaker to the UPS Main Distribution Board (UPS MDB).
  2. Push in the DIN rail clip to the DIN rail (see Figure 1).
  3. Reconnect all cables.
  4. Close the UPS MDB.
  5. Switch the power for the UPS MDB on.
