Information Bar Display

The information bar shows the parameters, location, status and operational information:

  • Date Time Group (DTG) (1)

  • Cursor location (2)

  • Distance between vehicle and home location (3)

  • Current task (4)

  • Mobile Common Operational Picture (COP) status (5)

  • Field unit status (6)

  • Radio network status (7)

  • Global Positioning System (GPS) status (8)

  • Distance between vehicle and target position (9)

  • Vehicle location (10)
