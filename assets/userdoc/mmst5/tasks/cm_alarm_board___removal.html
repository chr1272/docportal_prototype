Alarm Board - Removal

Removal of the Alarm Board.

  1. Shut off power to the HVAC Controller Panel at the circuit breaker.
  2. Open the HVAC Controller Panel (see item 6 in Figure 1).

  3. Note down the cabling.
  4. Remove all cables and connectors.
  5. Using a cross point screwdriver remove the 5 screws securing the board to the panel and remove the Alarm Board (see item 3 in Figure 2).
