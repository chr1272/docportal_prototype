Map Brightness Setting

To adjust the brightness of the map, carry out the following procedure.

Responsibility Field Unit Operator
Prerequisites N/A

  1. From the menu, press the button.

    The [BRIGHTNESS] dialog box is displayed.

  2. Move the cursor to adjust the Brightness.

  3. Press:

    • The [OK] button to validate the new brightness value.

    • The [RESET] button to reset the brightness to the previously saved value.
