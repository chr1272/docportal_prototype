Motor - Removal

Removal of the Motor.

  1. Open the Encoder Cover and empty the Gearbox of oil.
  2. Remove the Motor Cover and the two Side Covers.
  3. Open the Terminal Box (see Figure 1) and disconnect all cables.
    Figure 1. Motor
  4. Remove the nine bolts, the spacer and nut, and the washers, which fix the Motor to the Gearbox (see Figure 1).
  5. Remove the Motor.
