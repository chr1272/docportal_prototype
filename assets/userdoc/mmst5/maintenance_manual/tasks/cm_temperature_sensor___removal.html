Temperature Sensor - Removal

Removal of the Temperature Sensor.

  1. Shut off power to the HVAC Controller Panel at the circuit breaker.
  2. Open the HVAC Controller Panel (see item 6 in Figure 1).
    Figure 1. Ground Shelter Panels

  3. Note the position of the Temperature Sensor (see item 2 in Figure 2) at the bottom of the control panel.
  4. Pull out the Temperature Sensor (see item 1 in Figure 2).
    Figure 2. Temperature Sensor
    1
    Temperature Sensor, pulled out
    2
    Temperature Sensor, installed position
  5. Note the cable connections from the Temperature Sensor to the alarm board.
  6. Remove the cable to the Temperature Sensor from the clamp.
  7. Disconnect the cable from the alarm board.
  8. Pull the Temperature Sensor through the holder.
