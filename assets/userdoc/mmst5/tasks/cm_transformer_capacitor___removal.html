Transformer/Capacitor - Removal

Removal of the Transformer/Capacitor.

  1. Shut off power to the light fixture at the circuit breaker.
  2. To open the External Light remove the stainless-steel screws (see item 1 in Figure 1).

  3. Remove the Mercury bulb (see item 2 in Figure 1) by rotating the bulb in anti-clockwise direction.
  4. Remove the screws of the Aluminium Reflector (see item 1 to 3 in Figure 2) with an X-point screwdriver, small.

  5. Remove the Aluminium Reflector.
  6. Note down the cabling.
  7. Removal of the Capacitor
    1. Remove the Connector (see item 9 in Figure 2) from the Capacitor (see item 8 in Figure 2).
    2. Remove the screw (see item 7 in Figure 2) and the Capacitor.
  8. Removal of the Transformer
    1. Remove the cables from the clamp (see item 4 in Figure 2).
    2. Remove the screw (see item 6 in Figure 2) and the Transformer (see item 5 in Figure 2).
