Motion Detector - Removal

Removal of the Motion Detector.

  1. Switch the power for the Motion Detector at the Main Distribution Board (MDB) off.
  2. Remove the connection base cover with a Flat screwdriver, small.
  3. Note all cable connections to the Motion Detector (see item 5 in Figure 1).

  4. Remove the cable connection with an X-point screwdriver, small.
  5. Remove all cables.
  6. Remove the two screws, which secure the Motion Detector to the shelter body.
