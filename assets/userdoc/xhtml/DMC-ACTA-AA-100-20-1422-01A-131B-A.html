Acknowledge a Task Order (standard task)

Responsibility Field Unit Operator
Prerequisites A task order was sent by the headquarter
Check

The current task button in the information bar turns green and the acknowledgement is sent to the headquarter.

  1. Press the blinking letter to open the message. The [NEW STANDARD TASK] dialog box is displayed.

    It contains detailed information related to the task.

  2. Press the [CONFIRM] button to acknowledge the task. The [CONFIRM TASK] dialog box is displayed.

  3. Press the [OK] button to confirm.
