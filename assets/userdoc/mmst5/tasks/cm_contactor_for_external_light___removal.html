Contactor for External Light - Removal

Removal of the Contactor for External Light.

  1. Switch the power for the Main Distribution Board (MDB) off.
  2. Open the MDB to gain access to the Contactor for External Light.
  3. Note all cable connections to the Contactor for External Light.
  4. Remove the power cable of the Contactor for External Light IN and OUT.
  5. After removing the main cable and coil unit cable, unscrew the two screws (see item 1 in Figure 1) which fix the Contactor for External Light to the steel frame.
