UPS System - Troubleshooting

This troubleshooting procedure applies to failure at the UPS System in the Ground Shelter. The UPS System has both LED and audible signals that can help in troubleshooting a malfunction.

  2. If the UPS in on bypass mode then battery mode is not available and the equipment is not protected; however, the utility power continues to be passively filtered by the UPS. In this case check for one of the following alarms:

  • Overtemperature
  • Overload
  • UPS failure
  • Hot swap
  • REPO

  1. If bypass is unavailable, the UPS is receiving utility power that might be unstable or in brownout conditions. In this situation verify the bypass utility and then check for one of the following alarms:

  • Bypass over or under voltage
  • Bypass over or under frequency
  • Bypass unavailable
